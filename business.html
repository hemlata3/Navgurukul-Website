School of Business

Ever pictured yourself as a business person? Explore the world of business with skills in Google Suite, CRM, Digital Marketing, and Data Analytics, where we prepare you for a digital-first economy.

Who this Programme is for

  • Youth above 16 years of age from underprivileged or low-income communities who can't afford expensive college education
  • No prior education needed: Our students have been school and college dropouts, 10th or 12th pass as well as degree holders such as BSc, BTech who could not get jobs
  • Anyone from low-income families of less than 15,000 INR in urban areas and 10,000 INR in rural areas
  • Some level of English is good to have
  • Have heard about programming or have an interest in making a career in this field

What You Will Learn

Our student-led, self-paced 18-months programming course includes:

  1. Introduction to Google suite: Learning advanced Google Sheets, slides, and forms
  2. Introduction: Customer Relationship Management & Salesforce
  3. Digital Marketing: Website building, Social media marketing & SEO
  4. Data Analytics: Statistics, SQL queries, Tableau

co-curricular Activities : Apart from core subject learning student get to participate in voting for student council, running student councils, maintaining health & hygiene, cooking, sports, recreational & field visits

Admission Process

Screening Test

Assess fundamental skills up to 8th standard level in Mathematics, Logical Reasoning, and English Language.

Format: Online/Offline


Eligibility: Student from any stream


Test Structure and Marks:
Maths: 20 marks
English: 10 marks
Total: 30 marks


Passing Criteria:
Maths: 14 marks
English: 6 marks


Academic Interview

Evaluates 8th standard-level Math and English, similar to the screening test

Format: Online


Eligibility: Candidates qualifying the screening test


Culture Fit Round

Assess cultural alignment, focusing on needs, emotional aspects, and commitment to community

Format: Online/Offline


Eligibility: Successful candidates from the academic interview


Candidates are either marked 'Failed' or 'Preparation'. Depending on which re-interview is allowed in 7 to 15 days

Offer Letter

Confirms selection for residential course

Format: Online/Offline


Eligibility: Candidates passing the culture-fit round


Confirmation: Sent via mail
